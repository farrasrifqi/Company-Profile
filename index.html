About GTR

Nissan GT-R is one of the fastest cars in the world. In fact, because it is considered to have high performance and is fierce on the track, this car has earned the nickname Godzilla. This car uses a 6-inline engine with twin-turbocharged and has 4-wheel drive.

Becoming one of the supercars is not a short journey in this Nissan GT-R. This sports car turns 50 in 2019. Over the past 5 decades, Nissan has had six GT-R models since its premiere in 1969. Here's the history of Godzilla.
